NBA All-Star Influencer Campaign 2023

Overview

As the Marketing & Communications Lead for the NBA in Nigeria, I was responsible for spearheading strategic initiatives that enhanced brand engagement, digital growth, and cross-platform storytelling. My role focused on increasing visibility and fan interaction for the NBA All-Star 2023 campaign, managing digital and media partnerships, and promoting NBA digital assets such as NBA ID.

Strategy & Execution

  • Launched targeted acquisition campaigns across Instagram, Twitter, and YouTube, resulting in dynamic storytelling and viral content loops.
  • Partnered with local influencers and sports personalities to amplify fan-driven content.
  • Developed a retention-focused CX plan centered on push notifications, personalized email journeys, and in-app fan rewards.
  • Orchestrated high-impact brand activations at key events such as the 2023 NBA All-Star Weekend watch party.
  • Secured sponsorships with regional brands to extend campaign reach, including collaborative merchandise drops and co-branded digital content.
  • Deployed analytics dashboards to track campaign performance in real-time.

Tools

  • Hootsuite
  • Google Analytics
  • Adobe Creative Cloud
  • Sprinklr / native platform analytics

Results

  • 50% increase in social media followers, driven by consistent content cadence and influencer partnerships.
  • $2M estimated media value generated from the 2023 NBA All-Star Campaign.
  • 15% growth in engagement and viewership in Q1 2023 through CX and content personalization strategies.
  • 20% rise in media placements across local press and sports channels through high-impact PR and brand activations.
  • 15% increase in NBA ID digital subscriptions, with improved funnel conversion post-campaign.
